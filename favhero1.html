Introduction to prabhas

Prabhas is an Indian actor who works in Telugu cinema.
Prabhas is hailed as the "First Pan-Indian Superstar".
Born: October 23, 1979 · Madras, Tamil Nadu, India
Birth name:Prabhas Raju Uppalapati
Nicknames:DarlingRebel StarYoung Rebel Star
Height:6.5

family

Prabhas is the son of filmmaker U. Suryanarayana Raju and his wife Siva Kumari. He has two siblings: Prabodh Uppalapati his brother and Pragathi Uppalapati his sister.
He is the youngest of three siblings. Uppalapati Krishnam Raju a Telugu actor is his uncle.

Awards
